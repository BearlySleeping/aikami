The **Address System** controls how the AI Game Master addresses the player during conversations.

## Address Modes

Three modes available via the toggle at the bottom of the chat:

| Mode | Behavior |
|------|----------|
| **Scene** (default) | Omniscient third-person narrator. The GM describes the world without directly addressing the player. |
| **Party** | Multi-character group focus. Each party member speaks in their distinct voice, matching their personality traits. Responses include `**Name**: dialogue` formatting. |
| **GM** | Direct second-person address. The GM speaks to you as a human Game Master would. |

## Impersonation Drafting

The `/impersonate [direction]` slash command generates an in-character message as your player persona, based on recent chat context and your character sheet traits. The generated text appears in the input field for editing — **never auto-sent**.

Enable the 🎭 **Impersonate** toggle in chat settings to show a quick button next to the input. Clicking it generates a context-only draft ("what would my character do?").

If no active persona is set, a toast reminds you to complete your Character Sheet first.
